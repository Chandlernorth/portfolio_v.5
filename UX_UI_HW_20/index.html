Gamefest

Gamefest is a companion app to your game nights. It has dice, spinners, score cards, a game library, and a game randomizor for when you can't dececide on a game to play. With a modern and simple interface it is easier than ever to get started playing your favorite games! Have fun gamming!

The Problem

People are losing, breaking, or misplacing essential board game components. As a result, people are choosing to play other games, or are skipping playing games altogether.

The Solution

A board game companion app that allows users to use digital versions of popular game components, digital scoreboards, and random game selectors.

Research

Who: Young adults who host game nights for their friends or family.

What: 6 semi-structured zoom interviews

Research Objectives:

Determine what should be included in an app like Gamefest.

Determine the likelihood of Gamefest being utilized over another method.

Determine if the focus should be placed on in-person or remote use.

Determine the challenges related to family game night.

Determine the primary user(s) of the app in a game night setting.

Research Results

From those interviews we discovered that users are consistently losing essential game components, spend too much time choosing games to play, and wants a better way to keep score during their game nights.

We created a feature prioritization matrix with a few key features to focus on. Starting off with a way to keep score during their game, a way to see all of their games in the app, and choosing a random game to play for game nights.

Prototyping

Using figma we prototyped a solution with all of the insights that we gained from the research. Created a user centered prototype Created a clean, simple, yet fun design for the app Users were really excited about all of the features that Gamefest has to offer for their game nights.
